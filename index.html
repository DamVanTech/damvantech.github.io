About Me

Hi, I'm Will Hyndman, a student at Nova Scotia Community College in the IT Systems Management and Security program. I'm passionate about technology and am excited to build a career in IT.

I'm an enthusiastic and driven professional currently pursuing a Diploma in IT Systems Management and Security at Nova Scotia Community College. With a growing expertise in networking, system administration, and technical support, I'm focused on building a career in IT while leveraging my diverse background in customer service, food safety, and hands-on maintenance. I have hands-on experience working as an Information System Technician with the Nova Scotia Health Authority, where I supported IT infrastructure, managed networks, and provided technical support. In addition to my IT work, I've developed valuable customer service skills through various roles at McDonald's, Walmart, and The Wendy's Company, where I honed my ability to engage with customers and manage high-pressure situations effectively. Whether it's managing a network, assisting customers, or completing mechanical tasks, I pride myself on being adaptable and dedicated to continuous learning. I'm skilled in Windows Server, Active Directory, Python, HTML, and network administration, and am eager to apply my growing technical expertise in a professional setting.
